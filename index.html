LoFive

A tile game played by placing tiles onto a grid to earn points. By stacking tiles, the amounts on the tiles are added. These amounts must stay below 5, or else it’s game over! Filling rows and columns lower the tile values and earn more points. Players can unlock new color themes by reaching high scores.

Project Swirl

Having to drop out of Alchemy College to keep your family’s coffee shop from going out of business? Sucks. Awakening an ancient evil and defending the metropolitan area from an enchanted army of beverage monsters? Really sucks. Collect, mix, discover, and unleash the power of beverages in this quirky and nostalgic top-down action adventure.

Project Hoot

Play as Twinkle the Cat or Talon the Owl in this light-weight online co-op platformer where you work together to reach for the stars!
